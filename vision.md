Introduction
In a world where our professional lives are scattered across platforms, paper resumes, and unverifiable online claims, there is no single, trusted source that defines who we are, personally or professionally. their.Bio changes that.
their.Bio is a universal, trusted, and credible bio platform, built to become the new global standard for verified professional identity. It’s not just another profile platform; it’s your authenticated professional biography in the cloud , simple, minimal, and recognized everywhere.
Core Idea & Mission
Our mission is simple yet powerful: To eliminate paper CVs and create a digital ecosystem where people, companies, and institutions trust a single credible bio for every individual.
We envision a world where instead of sending a resume, people simply share their their.bio link, and companies, agencies, or universities instantly recognize it as authentic. The platform brings credibility to every profile through verified experiences, issued directly by companies or institutions.
their.Bio aims to become a household name, much like LinkedIn or Behance, but with one key difference: it’s built on verification and trust, not self-promotion.
How It Works
The platform experience is designed to be effortless and meaningful:
1. Create Your Bio – A user signs up as an individual or a company and builds their minimal, professional bio.
2. Get Sealed by Companies – A person can request a seal from companies, which they can approve. Companies can also visit any individual’s profile and issue a verified “Seal,” adding authentic experience details like roles, periods, and notes.
3. Share Your Bio – The individual’s bio becomes their universal digital identity, a link that replaces paper CVs forever.
Design & User Experience Philosophy
their.Bio follows a modern, minimal, and premium design philosophy, inspired by Apple’s simplicity and timeless aesthetics. The interface is clean, distraction-free, and built for credibility.
A person or company visiting the platform instantly understands its purpose: 'This is the world’s trusted place for verified professional bios.'
The homepage highlights credible and trending bios , both individuals and companies , celebrating trust, achievement, and integrity. It’s not social media; it’s identity media, built for authenticity and professionalism.
Monetization Concept
(Initially our platform is gonna be free for a specific time period)
We believe credibility shouldn’t be locked behind subscriptions. their.Bio uses a credit-based (pay-per-action) model, where users only pay for what they do.
Examples: Updating or verifying bio information costs minimal credits; requesting company seals or exporting a verified resume uses credits; companies can purchase credit packs for bulk seals or premium verification tools.
This system keeps the platform accessible for everyone while building a fair, scalable business model.

In the long term, we aim to collaborate with governments, universities, and international institutions to make their.Bio the internet’s most trusted professional identity standard.
